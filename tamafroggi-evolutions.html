EVOLUTION CHART & BESTIARY · egg → baby → junior → adult
evolution is automatic with age; the FORM you become is decided by your stats at the moment you evolve.
each evolved form grants a permanent stat bonus (shown in gold). babies are a free prep stage.
BESTIARY — ENEMIES
twelve archetypes, each with four tiers of sprite that scale as you descend the biomes — the last four are spellcasters.
every stage now ends in a boss; the regular foes below fill the run up to it.
BESTIARY — BIOME BOSSES
the true boss of each biome — the final foe of its last stage. huge unique sprites, guaranteed loot, and a signature mechanic layered on its dramatic specials.
ELITE MECHANICS
every stage ends in an elite. from Plains stage 3 onward each elite carries one of these ten gimmicks — Plains stages 1–2 stay plain for new players. the type is fixed per stage, so you can learn it and prepare.
SPELLS
cast automatically in combat — each equipped spell has its OWN cooldown and fires the moment it fills (there is no mana). you can equip up to 3 at once.
damage scales with INT, healing with SPIRIT; high INT+SPIRIT and haste gear shorten every cooldown. equipping more spells slows them all — 2 spells ×1.2, 3 spells ×1.4 cooldown — so you trade cast speed for utility (the legendary Triumvirate relic removes this). times below are base, with one spell equipped.
